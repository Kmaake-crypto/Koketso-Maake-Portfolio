About Me

I am Koketso Maake, a Full-Stack Web Developer student and professional Graphic Designer. I design and build digital experiences that are visually striking and functionally powerful. With a strong foundation in HTML and CSS, I create responsive, user-focused websites while crafting bold, memorable graphic designs that communicate brand identity with clarity and impact.

My work sits at the intersection of creativity and technology—where clean code meets impactful design. I focus on building intuitive user interfaces, responsive layouts, and visually compelling designs that elevate brands and enhance user experience.
